3D Printer

As an Americorps member at Boys and Girls Clubs of Acadiana, I

  • fixed up their Ultimaker S5 for use (replacing fans, bed leveling, cleaning),
  • started a 200+ student class on TinkerCAD to teach 3D modeling,
  • taught students to make and modify designs,
  • documented and taught the process for other Americorps members and employees.,

and printed out students' designs...sometimes after some slight modifications to make sure that they wouldn't fail.



It was one of the most exhausting and rewarding experiences that I've ever had- it taught me that I don't have the skills or inclination for classroom management.
