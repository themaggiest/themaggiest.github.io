Prom Dress

In Louisiana, it's traditional for girls to wear white dresses to their senior prom. To me, white is a color that remembers things - so I decided to make a dress to commemorate my high school experience. I brought fabric markers to prom and asked everyone to sign my dress, which I made by

  • reverse engineering patterns from pieces of clothing that fit me,
  • cutting them out with extra seam allowance from old bedsheets,
  • modifying the designs as necessary based on my measurements,
  • asking my wonderfully artistic friend to paint on the bodice fabric,
  • recording the patterns for future use,

and sewing the dress together using my family's sewing machine.
